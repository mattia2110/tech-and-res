# Tech & Res , Victoria 3 Mod

A Victoria 3 mod that extends the technology tree, production methods, and related mechanics 
after 1936 into later eras (atomic age, digital age, and beyond).

## Workflow Guidelines
1. The directory of Victoria 3 is in this path: C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Victoria 3\game\
2. The skills that you can use are in this path: .claude\skills\.
3. Do not interact with Git, it is not needed.
4. When you have finished a task, append a summary at the end of LOG.md and delete last week's summary.

## Sub-Agents
You can call the following sub-agents to help with specific tasks:
- `localization-writer`: for writing and improving English localization while preserving script correctness and mod terminology.
You can also create sub-agents as needed to help with specific tasks, but make sure to document their purpose.
